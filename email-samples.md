# Email Samples Analyzed

## Sample 1: Phishing Email
**From:** security@paypal-verify.com  
**Subject:** URGENT: Your Account Has Been Limited

Dear Customer,

We've detected suspicious activity on your PayPal account. 
To avoid permanent suspension, verify your identity immediately:
http://paypal-security-verify.com/account/restore

Failure to respond within 24 hours will result in account closure.

- PayPal Security Team

---

## Sample 2: Suspicious Email
**From:** support@amazon-customer-service.net  
**Subject:** Your Order #ORD-38492 Cannot Be Processed

Hello,

Your recent order cannot be processed due to payment issues.
Please confirm your billing information:
https://amazon-payment-verify.com/billing

Amazon Customer Service

---

## Sample 3: Safe Email
**From:** noreply@amazon.com  
**Subject:** Your Amazon Order Confirmation #113-1234567-8901234

Hello John,

Thank you for your order. We'll let you know when your items ship.

Order Details:
- Wireless Headphones (1) - $49.99

View your order: https://www.amazon.com/orders/113-1234567-8901234

Questions? Visit our Help Desk at amazon.com/help

- Amazon Customer Service
