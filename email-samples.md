# Email Samples Analyzed

## Sample 1: Phishing Email (High Risk) - PayPal Account Scam

**From:** security@paypal-verify.com  
**Subject:** ⚠️ URGENT: Your PayPal Account Has Been Permanently Restricted

Dear Valued Customer,

We have detected multiple failed login attempts and unusual activity associated with your PayPal account from an unrecognized device. As a security measure, your account has been temporarily limited and is scheduled for permanent suspension within the next 24 hours.

To avoid losing access to your funds and payment features, you must verify your identity immediately by clicking the secure link below:

➡️ http://paypal-security-verify.com/account/restore/verify-identity

Once verified, your account will be restored within 2 hours. If we do not receive verification within 24 hours, your account will be permanently closed and all pending transactions will be canceled.

We apologize for any inconvenience this may cause. Thank you for being a valued PayPal customer.

Sincerely,  
The PayPal Security Team

## Sample 2: Phishing Email (High Risk) - CEO Fraud / Wire Transfer Scam

**From:** james.carter@company-domain-support.com  
**Subject:** RE: Urgent Wire Transfer Request - Authorize Immediately

Hello Team,

I am currently in a high-level client meeting and cannot be reached by phone. I need your immediate assistance with a time-sensitive matter.

We have finalized a new vendor agreement with Summit Partners International, and they require an advance payment of $48,500 today to secure the contract terms. The deadline is in the next 2 hours, otherwise we risk losing this opportunity.

Please process the wire transfer to the following account details:

**Bank:** Wells Fargo  
**Account Name:** Summit Partners International LLC  
**Account Number:** 879203451  
**Routing Number:** 121000248  
**Amount:** $48,500.00

I need confirmation as soon as this is completed. Please reply to this email with the confirmation number once done.

I will confirm with the client once you have sent the confirmation.

Thank you for handling this urgently.

Best,  
James Carter  
Chief Executive Officer

**From:** invoices@global-suppliers-network.org  
**Subject:** Invoice #INV-38492-2026 - Payment Overdue

Dear Accounts Payable,

This is a reminder that invoice #INV-38492-2026 in the amount of $12,847.50 is now past due. Our records show that payment has not been received, and the balance remains outstanding.

**Invoice Details:**
- **Invoice Number:** INV-38492-2026
- **Issue Date:** February 15, 2026
- **Due Date:** March 1, 2026
- **Amount Due:** $12,847.50
- **Service:** IT Infrastructure Maintenance (Q1 2026)

Please note that a 5% late fee will be applied to any unpaid balances after March 15, 2026. If payment has already been sent, please disregard this notice.

To view the full invoice and payment instructions, please click the link below:

➡️ http://global-suppliers-network.org/invoices/INV-38492-2026/view

For any questions regarding this invoice, please contact our billing department at billing@global-suppliers-network.org.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Regards,  
Maria Santos  
Accounts Receivable  
Global Suppliers Network
